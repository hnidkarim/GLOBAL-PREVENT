Methodology

We do not collect raw U.S. variables (cholesterol, SBP, etc.) in this app. The calculator implements the international correction only:

P_c(τ) = 1 − [S₀c(τ)]^exp( γc · [η_US − μc] )

  • S₀c(10), S₀c(30): country-specific baseline survival
  • μc: mean LP in the local population
  • γc: calibration slope from a local Cox model vs ηUS

If you already know ηUS for a patient (e.g., exported from PREVENT™), paste it in LP. Otherwise, use presets and adjust.
